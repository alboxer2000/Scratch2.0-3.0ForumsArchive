    Actually, on the old site, My Stuff used to be what the “profile” is now. Now it means a place that only you can see, where you can organize your projects (shared or unshared). So nobody can see anyone else's my stuff. It's sort of more literally “my stuff”.

    What are your first impressions?

    angelica101
    Awesome!! Thanks for letting me check it out! One question- How do I delete a gallery, and how do I export a sound, but not on a local file?? And how can I reverse a song?
    I don't think deleting galleries has been added yet - but I'm sure they'll add that before the final release. You can export a sound by going to the “sounds” category, and then right clicking on the sound icon, and select “export to local file”. You can reverse a sound by selecting “edit” > “select all”, and then “effects” > “reverse”. That's what seems to do the job best - tell me if it doesn't work.
